The Culture Tent

The Culture Tent

The CultureTent is a non-profit summer program for Syrian refugee and under-resourced youth in south Lebanon.

What started in 2016 as a simple supply drop - blankets, socks, rice - became a week of songs and games, then a staffed program with age-grouped classes in spoken Arabic/English, reading, basic math, and creative workshops (such as drawing, music, storytelling). We bring simple materials, including books, puzzles, workbooks, shared e-learning tablets, and coordinate with local families so kids get structured learning and a safe place to play when public school access is limited.

Since 2016 we've raised $20k+ in small donations, recruited ~15 volunteers, and returned each summer with a lean, repeatable curriculum and local staffing. To support the program, I ran the Brooklyn Marathon in 2021 to fundraise and expand supplies. The principle is straightforward: ask first, then build what's missingâ€”education, self-expression, and a little joy.
